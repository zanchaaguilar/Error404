About Us

Launched in August 2004, Star Bus is today ranked as the leading provider of consumer-direct travel services in the Philippines. It has emerged as the most trusted travel brand in the Philippines and has won three awards at the Philippines Tourism Awards felicitated for "Outstanding Performance as a Domestic Tour Operator (Rest of Philippines)", amongst many others over the years. Based in Camarines Sur, Philippines, Star Bus is a one-stop-shop for all travel-related services. A leading consolidator of travel products, EZ Seat provides reservation facility around the world. Through continued excellence in providing travel solutions, responses to booking travel online have also reached new heights with the company doing 20,000 domestic tickets and holiday packages a day. Positioned as a brand that believes in "Creating Happy Travelers", Star Bus provides information, pricing, availability, and booking facility for domestic purposes. Customers can access Star Bus through multiple ways: through our user-friendly website, 24x7 multi-lingual call center, a countrywide network of Holiday Lounges and Star Bus Express stores, Star Bus provides a booking facility for all the popular as well as exotic national destinations. With the developing tourism in Southern Luzon, including the expanding market of the local products and delicacies, the company is well-positioned to be part of this growth. Our passengers can take advantage of the many services we provide one of which is our hassle-free chartered trips that bring tourists and locals to and from hotels, resorts and the beautiful islands of the Southern Region. The timely and efficient delivery of local products, baggage and cargo to the Southern Region forms as part of our Company thrusts. We, at Star Bus, are committed to providing only the best services to the Filipino people. Backed up by 15 years of experience in the business, you can be assured that we bring you home safer.
